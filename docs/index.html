ECHOES

Equal-weight Completed Hypothetical Observation Ensembles

ECHOES turns a spectroscopic galaxy survey into an ensemble of equal-weight, cosmology-free completed catalogs: observed galaxies are kept, and the spectroscopically-missing galaxies are restored at their real imaging positions with data-driven redshifts. Run any clustering statistic — two-point, higher-order, marked, nearest-neighbor, topological, field-level — directly on completed point catalogs and propagate the completion uncertainty by resampling. The first release completes BOSS DR12 CMASS-South.
